Build a Databricks or Snowflake lakehouse with tested dbt models and automated ingestion.

We connect ERP systems (SAP, NetSuite, Sage), CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), and operational databases into a single governed lakehouse on AWS, Azure, or GCP.

  • Automated ingestion pipelines orchestrated with Airflow, pulling from REST APIs, SFTP feeds, databases, and SaaS platforms on a defined schedule.
  • dbt tests and data quality checks on every pipeline run, alerting your team to anomalies before they reach dashboards.
  • Governed semantic layers in Power BI or Looker that standardize KPI definitions across finance, operations, and sales.

The deliverable is a production data platform with CI/CD deployment, monitoring, and documentation — typically operational within 8–12 weeks.

Deploy Azure OpenAI-powered automation with RAG pipelines and human-in-the-loop controls.

We identify your highest-friction manual workflows — invoice processing, contract review, support ticket triage — and build LLM-powered automation using Azure OpenAI, vector stores, and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG).

Delivered in Agile 2-week sprints with pilot-to-production timelines of 6–10 weeks.

  • Invoice extraction, contract clause identification, and support ticket classification using structured LLM output parsing.
  • Human-in-the-loop approval gates to maintain compliance and quality at every step.
  • Performance dashboards that track cycle-time reduction, accuracy rates, and ROI per automation.
  • Private deployment on Azure with role-based access, prompt logging, and cost monitoring — no data leaves your tenant.

Replace spreadsheet reporting with governed Power BI, Tableau, or Looker dashboards.

We build reporting around the decisions your team actually makes — executive scorecards, operational dashboards, and embedded forecasting models.

  • Executive scorecards in Power BI or Tableau with drill-through to operational drivers and row-level security.
  • Demand forecasting and planning models built in Python or Databricks ML, surfaced directly in your BI tool.
  • Automated alerting and anomaly detection that surfaces risk before it hits your P&L.

For healthcare clients, that means patient volume and revenue cycle dashboards refreshing hourly. For agriculture, yield-per-acre tracking by field. No more Monday-morning spreadsheet scrambles.
