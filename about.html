About DataLeap

Meet Patel

Founder & Principal Consultant

A boutique consultancy built on hands-on expertise

DataLeap is a boutique data engineering and AI consultancy founded by Meet Patel and based in Sioux Falls, South Dakota. We partner with operations and finance leaders in healthcare, agriculture, financial services, and manufacturing to replace fragmented spreadsheets and manual reporting with automated, tested data pipelines.

Meet brings deep experience across the full data lifecycle — from designing cloud-native architectures and reliable ETL/ELT pipelines to deploying production AI solutions including LLM integrations, RAG pipelines, and automation workflows. His work spans platforms like Databricks, Snowflake, dbt, and Azure, helping growth-stage and enterprise teams turn data into their most powerful strategic asset.

Before founding DataLeap, Meet honed his craft across healthcare systems, agricultural operations, financial services firms, and manufacturers across the Midwest — building the technical foundation and client-facing skills that define the consultancy's approach today: practical, results-driven, and backed by structured knowledge transfer so clients operate independently within 90 days.

As a focused, founder-led firm, DataLeap offers the senior-level attention and accountability that larger consultancies often cannot. Every engagement is led directly by Meet, ensuring hands-on delivery, clear communication, and outcomes aligned to your business goals.

Core Expertise

Data Platform Engineering

Databricks and Snowflake lakehouses, dbt transformation pipelines with automated testing, and Airflow-orchestrated ingestion from ERP, CRM, and operational systems.

AI Workflow Automation

Azure OpenAI-powered document processing, RAG pipelines over internal knowledge bases, and LLM integrations embedded into existing Microsoft 365 and CRM workflows.

Advanced Analytics

Power BI, Tableau, and Looker dashboards backed by governed semantic models — replacing spreadsheet consolidation with self-service reporting and demand forecasting.

Change Enablement

Hands-on training workshops, runbook documentation, and a 90-day post-launch support period with weekly office hours so your team operates independently.
